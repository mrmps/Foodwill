About Us...

A solution to Food Waste

Lets say you have extra fruits in your backyard...

But they are going to waste because you don't have spaces to store them.

Here is where Foodwill comes in.

Foodwill helps you to find homes for these extra food, making sure that they aren't going to waste. Leave the extra food on your porch so that people can easily pick them up.

Money for good a cause.

Buyers pay a reasonable price for these food and part of it goes to charities to make this world a better place.

Why are we doing this?

We have a food wasting crisis in the US, over a third of perfectly good food goes to waste. But at the same time, 1 in 8 Americans are going hungry, that's over 41 million people. The causes of this are shocking. For example, grocery stores overstock items because they believe having a fully stock store shelf and healthy looking grocery that's far off the expiration date are beneficial to the sales of the store, but that's also wasting plenty of food. Foodwill is the best way to connect people who are hungry and people who doesn't know what to do with their extra food. There's an old saying, 'one man's trash is another man's treasure,', it all depends on how we utilize this so called trash to become the treature.
